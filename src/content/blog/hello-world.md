I've been meaning to start writing publicly for a while. This is the first post — partly to break the seal, partly to test that everything renders the way I want.

## What this is

A place for notes on three things I spend most of my time thinking about:

- **AI** — how the models actually behave, what's useful, what's hype.
- **Security** — applied appsec, threat modeling, and things I learn the hard way.
- **Web performance** — making the web fast, and the surprisingly deep rabbit holes that come with it.

Some posts will be polished essays. Most will be working notes — the kind of thing I write down so I don't have to solve the same problem twice.

## Why bother

> Writing is nature's way of letting you know how sloppy your thinking is.

Explaining something forces me to actually understand it. If a post is useful to someone else too, that's a bonus.
